## Architecting the Future in the Cloud.

   **Founder - Jeff Kessie**

I am the Founder of a cloud consulting firm, where I drive innovation strategy, cloud transformation, and secure infrastructure services. I am committed to delivering full life cycle solutions that enable organizations to maximize the value of their technology investments while maintaining the highest standards of security and performance.

Previously, I served at Booz Allen Hamilton, where I led a major cloud migration initiative, transitioning more than 100 legacy systems to modern cloud platforms while implementing a Zero Trust security architecture. I also held a key role at Amazon Web Services (AWS), where I developed automation solutions leveraging tools such as AWS CloudFormation and Pulumi to accelerate and streamline service delivery.

I hold Department of Defense IAT Level III certifications and bring extensive expertise in cloud architecture, automation, and secure systems design for both public and private sector clients. I earned a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology from George Mason University.
